Help

  • You can improve your knowledge easily by playing the quiz. There will be a total of ten categories in Gyaan Vruttant Quiz.

  • By clicking on the "Start Quiz" button, you will be directed to the categories page, where you will be given several categories. You can choose any category of your choice.

  • In every category, you will get three levels.

  • In each category, your quiz will start from Level 1. You will reach Level 2 only if you get at least 3 marks in Level 1 and Level 3 only if you get at least 3 marks in Level 2. This means the passing number for each level is 3.

  • All our levels have 7 questions. Each of them is a multiple-choice question. You must choose the correct answer to win the quiz.

  • After solving all the questions, click on the "Submit" button to view your marks.

  • There is a "Reset" button to reset all your responses.

  • You can also save your data in Gyaan Vruttant Quiz by clicking on the "Save" button. This feature is not working now but will start working in a few days.

  • You can go to the next level by clicking on the "Next Level" button.

  • If you would like to know more about us, you can go to our About Us page by clicking "About Us" in the navigation bar, where you can read about our goals and mission.

  • You can provide feedback by clicking "Feedback" in the navigation bar and filling out and submitting the feedback form.

  • You can sign up and log in by clicking on "Sign up" and "Login" in the navigation bar. First, you need to create an account by signing up, then log in to your account. You will go to your personal dashboard where your name, email, and quiz marks will be shown along with the level and category. When you want to log out, click on "Logout".
