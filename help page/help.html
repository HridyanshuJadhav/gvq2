Help

Welcome to the Gyan Vruttant Quiz

    You can improve your knowledge easily by play the quiz. There will be total 5 levels in Gyan Vruttant Quiz.

  • By clicking of the start button you will reach on first level of quiz.

  • You will be given 20 questions in each level and given 4 options in each question.

  • You have to select the correct option to win the quiz.

  • All the question in our quiz are multiple choice, so you have to select only one option.

  • Afrer attempting questions you will click in the submit button to see the result.

  • You will only jump to next level when you clear the first one and first level qualifying marks is at least 8.

  • If you have less than 8 marks you will not be able to jump to next level.

  • After qualifying level 1 you will reach on level 2 and to qualify level 2 also you need to have at least 8 marks and after level 2 you will reach on level 3 and so on.

    • You have great experience after qialifing all the levels of Gyan Vruttant Quiz. I hope you will have a great expeirence with playing quiz.
